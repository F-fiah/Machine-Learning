# 逻辑回归

识别图像，判断图像中是否存在目标物体
1. 讲图像矩阵转换成向量
2. 通过 sigmoid 函数把向量转换成一个函数值 $\hat y = \sigma(wx+b)$
3. initiate w and b
4. **get the optimal w and b**（最大似然估计+梯度下降）
5. predict
$$ \begin{aligned} \text{neuron} &= \text{linear} + \text{activation} \\ \text{model} &= \text{architecture} + \text{parameter} \end{aligned} $$
$a_i^{[j]}$：第 j 层的第 i 个神经元 
$w_i^{[j]} x+b_i^{[j]} \quad \text{记为} \quad z_i^{[j]}$

**如何训练参数：反向传播**
- $\mathcal{L}^{(i)}$：loss，第 $i$ 个样本单独的损失
- $J$：cost，整个数据集所有样本损失的平均值
$$ J(\hat{y},y) = \frac{1}{m}\sum_{i=1}^{m} \mathcal{L}^{(i)} $$
$$ \mathcal{L}^{(i)} = -\left[ y^{(i)}\log\hat{y}^{(i)} + \left(1 - y^{(i)}\right)\log\left(1 - \hat{y}^{(i)}\right) \right] $$
For example :
$$ \begin{aligned} z^{(1)} &= W^{(1)}x + b^{(1)}, & a^{(1)} &= \sigma(z^{(1)}) \\ z^{(2)} &= W^{(2)}a^{(1)} + b^{(2)}, & a^{(2)} &= \sigma(z^{(2)}) \\ z^{(3)} &= W^{(3)}a^{(2)} + b^{(3)}, & \hat{y} &= a^{(3)} = \sigma(z^{(3)}) \end{aligned} $$
训练过程中，将所有 z 和 a 都记录下来

1. 通过梯度法计算最后一层的误差 $\delta$，然后不断向前传递
	1. $\delta^{(l)} = \dfrac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial z^{(l)}}$
	2. 先计算出输出层误差 $\delta^{(3)}$，只依赖**模型预测值 $\hat{y}$ 和真实标签$y$**
	3. $\delta^{(2)} = \dfrac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial z^{(2)}} = \underbrace{\dfrac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial z^{(3)}}}_{\delta^{(3)}} \cdot \underbrace{\dfrac{\partial z^{(3)}}{\partial a^{(2)}}}_{W^{(3)}} \cdot \underbrace{\dfrac{\partial a^{(2)}}{\partial z^{(2)}}}_{\sigma'(z^{(2)})}$，根据 $\delta^{(3)}$ 计算  $\delta^{(2)}$
	4. 同理，计算 $\delta^{(1)}$
2. 用 $\delta$ 算出每层 W、b 的梯度$$ \begin{aligned} \frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial W^{(3)}} &= \delta^{(3)} \cdot \left(a^{(2)}\right)^T, & \frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial b^{(3)}} &= \delta^{(3)} \\[4pt] \frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial W^{(2)}} &= \delta^{(2)} \cdot \left(a^{(1)}\right)^T, & \frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial b^{(2)}} &= \delta^{(2)} \\[4pt] \frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial W^{(1)}} &= \delta^{(1)} \cdot x^T, & \frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial b^{(1)}} &= \delta^{(1)} \end{aligned} $$
3. 用梯度下降公式，沿着梯度反方向更新全部参数$$ \begin{aligned} W^{(l)} &= W^{(l)} - \eta \cdot \frac{\partial J}{\partial W^{(l)}} \\ b^{(l)} &= b^{(l)} - \eta \cdot \frac{\partial J}{\partial b^{(l)}} \end{aligned} $$
